About Face2ID...

Face2ID revolutionizes patient identification in hospitals, especially for those without official IDs. This system uses facial recognition technology to generate a unique biometric hash for each patient. This contactless and user-friendly approach reduces human error and manipulation risks associated with traditional methods like recording names and birth dates. Face2ID ensures a more secure and efficient identification process, enhancing the reliability of healthcare systems.
